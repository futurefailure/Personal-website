About Capybaras

Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world. They are native to South America and are known for their social and friendly nature.

Habitat

Capybaras live in densely forested areas near bodies of water, such as rivers, lakes, swamps, and marshes.

Diet

Capybaras are herbivores and primarily eat grasses and aquatic plants. They also eat fruit and tree bark occasionally.
